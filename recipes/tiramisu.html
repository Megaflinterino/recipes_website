Tiramisu recipe

Description

A classic Italian dessert featuring layers of soft mascarpone cheese, coffee-soaked ladyfingers, and a dusting of cocoa powder.

Known for its creamy texture and rich coffee flavor, tiramisu is a true indulgence often enjoyed at the end of a meal.

ingredients

  • mascarpone
  • Yellow of the egg
  • Sugar
  • savoiardi
  • Espresso
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. mix the eggs with the sugar
  2. add mascarpone and whisk
  3. cover the bottom of a adequate form with the mixture
  4. add in espresso soaked savoiardi
  5. top with the mixture and repeat till nothing of the mixture or saviardie are left
  6. lightly cover with cocoa Powder
