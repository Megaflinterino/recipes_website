Taco recipe

Description

A traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients like meat, beans, vegetables, and salsa.

Tacos are versatile and can be prepared in countless ways depending on regional preferences, making them a popular street food.

ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • ground beef
  • onions
  • lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Self made guacamole

Steps

  1. cook the ground beef and season it well
  2. Dice the onions and cut the lettuce and the tomatos
  3. add the cooked ground beef and the vegetableswith the Guacamole in your taco shell
