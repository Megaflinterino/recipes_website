Pizza recipe

Description

An Italian dish made with a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese,

and a variety of toppings such as vegetables, meats, or seafood.

Pizza comes in many styles and delights with its combination of savory flavors and crispy texture.

ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • toppings of your choice
  • Mozarella cheese
  • dried Oregano

steps

  1. first spread the tomato sauce on the flat dough
  2. Add the toppings of your choosing
  3. Sprinkle the shredded Mozarella cheese over the pizza
  4. add some of the dried Oregano
  5. Put the pizza in the middle in the oven at 200Â° for around 20 minutes
